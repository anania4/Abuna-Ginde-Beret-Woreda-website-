Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I get my Resident ID?

You can visit your local Kebele administration office with proof of residence and two passport-sized photos during working hours.

What are the market days in Beke Kelate?

The main market day is Saturday, with smaller markets on Tuesdays.

How do I report a water line breakage?
